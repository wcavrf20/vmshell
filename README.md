## Why CMIN2 Even Matters

If you're using China Mobile broadband and you've ever tried a regular overseas VPS, you know the pain. Packet loss spikes in the evening, videos buffering, ping times that would make a dial-up modem blush.

CMIN2 (China Mobile International Network 2, AS58807) is China Mobile's high-end international transit line — essentially the express lane between Hong Kong and the mainland. For mobile broadband users, it's the best option outside of CN2 GIA, with genuinely low packet loss and solid performance during peak hours. VmShell's Hong Kong data center runs on CMI infrastructure, which is what makes their HK products interesting for this use case.
